About KZN Aquatics

Our Mission

To develop, promote, and regulate aquatic sports in KwaZulu-Natal, providing opportunities for all to participate and excel in a safe, inclusive, and competitive environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading provincial sports federation recognized for excellence in athlete development, competition organization, and governance of aquatic sports.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Inclusivity, Safety, and Development. These values guide all our programs, competitions, and administrative processes.
